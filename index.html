Our Mission

To create an organization that educates the public and promotes the concept of plastic-free commerce and acts to displace as much toxic petrochemical based plastic packaging as possible with non toxic eco-friendly alternatives and dramatically reduce the burden plastic waste places on the environment's creature and future generations.

Here's how

1

Inform the public, using all forms of multimedia resources, that they can help change the world through their choices and going plastic free is a big important step!

2

Identify suppliers of alternative packaging options and help connect them with potential users.

3

Educate manufacturers about the benefits of choosing sustainable eco alternatives.

4

Create an easily identifiable symbol and certification, validation that will allow consumers to recognize plastic-free product options.

5

Create public facing events, advertisements, PR and PSAs that celebrate companies that join the PFPA and invite others to do the same.

6

Create a website with a search engine to help consumers find our members and manufactures discover the solutions and resources to transition to plastic-free packaging.

7

Hold ECO fundraisers to promote the cause and showcase plastic-free initiatives.

8

Align with other Business and Public organizations such as American Sustainable Business Association, Surfrider Foundation, and Friends of the Earth to co-promote.

9

Track our progress and Impacts in industry sectors with a tonnage saved metrics, and other valid Measurement systems.

10

Explore and form relationships with governmental agencies that can support and incentivize this transition to plastic free packaging
